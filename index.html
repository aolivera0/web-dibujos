Comisiones

Commissioning an original artwork is a personal and meaningful experience, and an opportunity to own a custom, one-of-a-kind piece of art. Darren is pleased to be able to share his passion professionally and accepts public and private commissions worldwide.

COMMISSION SUBJECTS

Reasons for commissioning an artwork are many and varied. Darren works closely and collaboratively with clients and collectors to bring their vision to life in his unique aesthetic, recognisable style and interpretation. Commissions focussing on any of the subjects and themes reflected in his repertoire are welcome.

PET PORTRAITS

Pet portraits are a frequently sought commission and beautiful way to celebrate the life of a pet or companion animal. From large canines to small invertebrates, Darren’s exquisite portraits capture the spirit and personality of each animal, resulting in a truly special keepsake. A portion of proceeds from all commissioned pet portraits benefits a no-kill animal shelter.

MATERIALS

Darren works exclusively with the finest, professional quality artist’s materials. Black and white works are completed with graphite, charcoal and ink. Colour works are completed with graphite, watercolour, coloured pencil and ink. All works are completed on Fabriano Artistico hot-pressed watercolour paper, a 100% cotton, mould-made paper from Italy.
